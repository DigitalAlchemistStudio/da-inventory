Templates & Resources

Download a print-ready template in your format of choice, design inside the green safe area and extend art out to the red bleed, then attach your finished artwork when you submit a product. Every template is 300 DPI and CMYK-ready. The Fits line shows which products & sizes each one covers. Affinity & CSP masters are supplied by a manager — those buttons activate once the file is uploaded.

🔄 Shopify Sync Shopify = source of truth

This desktop tool is where you build products and generate SKUs. Your live stock, orders and prices live in Shopify. Sync runs securely through Claude — nothing here stores a Shopify password or API key.
⬆️ Push to Shopify
Click Push → Shopify CSV above to export a Shopify-ready file. Then either upload it in Shopify Admin → Products → Import, or just tell Claude “push my inventory CSV to Shopify.”
⬇️ Pull from Shopify
Ask Claude “pull my live Shopify inventory” and import the CSV it gives you via Import CSV. Rows are matched by SKU — quantities and prices update in place, no duplicates.
📱 On iOS
Install the free Shopify app from the App Store and sign in. Because Shopify holds the master data, edits you make on your phone and here both reconcile against the same store.

💸 Artist payouts

Each artist earns their share of the profit (sale price − manufacture cost). Default 40% to the artist, 60% to Digital Alchemists. Change a rate and it applies to that artist's earnings everywhere.
